## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end **E-Commerce Sales Analysis** using the **Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Dataset**. The project combines **MySQL** for data cleaning, transformation, and business analysis with **Power BI** for building an interactive dashboard that helps analyze sales performance, customer behavior, payment trends, and regional performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Understand customer purchasing behavior.
* Analyze preferred payment methods.
* Identify high-performing states based on revenue.
* Track order status distribution.
* Build an interactive business dashboard for decision-making.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

* Credit Card is the most preferred payment method among customers.
* São Paulo (SP) generates the highest overall revenue.
* A small number of product categories contribute significantly to total sales.
* Most customer orders are successfully delivered.
* Monthly revenue shows noticeable fluctuations throughout the year.

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Increase marketing efforts in top-performing states.
* Promote high-revenue product categories through targeted campaigns.
* Improve logistics performance in lower-performing regions.
* Encourage customers to use digital payment methods through promotional offers.
* Monitor monthly sales trends for better inventory and demand planning.
